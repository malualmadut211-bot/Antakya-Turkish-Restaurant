Our Story

From Antakya to Juba

Bringing authentic Turkish flavors to the heart of South Sudan. A culinary journey bridging two vibrant cultures through taste, tradition, and hospitality.

A Bridge Between Cultures

Founded with a passion for the rich culinary traditions of Antakya (Hatay), a UNESCO City of Gastronomy, our restaurant stands as a testament to the enduring power of food to connect people.

Our authentic Turkish chefs bring generations of heritage to every dish, crafting a premium dining experience that combines the warmth of Turkish hospitality with the vibrant, growing energy of Juba. Whether you are seeking the comfort of a stone-oven Lahmacun or the delicate layers of handmade Baklava, you are tasting history.
